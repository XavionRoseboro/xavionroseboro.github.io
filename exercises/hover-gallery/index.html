Spongebob

follows the misadventures of SpongeBob, a yellow sea sponge who lives in Bikini Bottom, and his friends.

Regular Show

Two best friends, a blue jay named Mordecai and a brown raccoon named Rigby, work as groundskeepers at a park, spending their days trying to entertain themselves while trying to deal with their by-the-book boss.

Power Puff Girls

The Powerpuff Girls is an animated superhero television series featuring three kindergarten-aged girls, Blossom, Bubbles, and Buttercup, who possess superpowers and protect their town, Townsville, from various villains.

Ben 10

The animated series "Ben 10" follows the adventures of 10-year-old Ben Tennyson, who discovers an alien watch called the Omnitrix.
